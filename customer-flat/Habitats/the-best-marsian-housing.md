# The Best Marsian Housing

## Introduction

Welcome to the definitive guide on Marsian housing concepts.
This document explores the most innovative and sustainable
housing solutions designed for the red planet.

## Design Principles

### Radiation Shielding

All Marsian habitats must provide adequate protection against
cosmic radiation and solar particle events. The recommended
minimum shielding is equivalent to 50 cm of Martian regolith.

### Pressurisation

Interior pressure is maintained at 70 kPa (approximately 70%
of Earth sea-level pressure) to reduce structural stress while
remaining comfortable for inhabitants.

### Thermal Management

Mars surface temperatures range from −143 °C to 35 °C. Active
and passive thermal regulation systems are essential.

The best Marsian housing combines safety, comfort and scalability.
Future iterations will integrate in-situ resource utilisation (ISRU)
to reduce dependence on Earth-based supply chains.
