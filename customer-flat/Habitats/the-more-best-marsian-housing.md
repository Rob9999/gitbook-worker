# The More Best Marsian Housing

## Beyond the Basics

Building on the foundations established in *The Best Marsian Housing*,
this document explores next-generation habitat designs that push the
boundaries of Martian architecture.

## Advanced Concepts

### Bio-Regenerative Life Support

Integrated greenhouses and algae bioreactors provide:

- 🌱 Fresh food production (up to 40% of caloric needs)
- 🫁 Oxygen generation via photosynthesis
- 💧 Grey-water recycling and purification
- 🧠 Psychological well-being through green spaces

### Modular Expansion

Each habitat unit connects via standardised airlocks (ISO-MARS-2031),
allowing communities to grow organically.
